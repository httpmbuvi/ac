Frequently Asked Questions

What is the turnaround time for a website?

A standard website (like our Bronze or Silver package) typically takes 1-2 weeks from start to finish. More complex projects, like e-commerce sites or custom systems, can take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline in our project proposal.

Do you provide hosting and domain registration?

Yes, all of our web development packages include one year of free domain registration and web hosting to get you started without any hassle.

What are your payment terms?

We require a 60% deposit to commence work on any project. The remaining 40% balance is due upon project completion and before the final website or system goes live.
